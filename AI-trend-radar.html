📡 AI Trend Radar

AI-powered system that detects trends, patterns, and business opportunities from large datasets automatically.

OVERVIEW

📊 What this system does

This system analyzes large sets of articles stored in a database and uses AI to identify patterns, trends, and emerging topics.

Instead of manually scanning content, businesses receive structured intelligence including trends, key topics, and opportunities — automatically generated.

PROCESS

⚙️ How it works

  • Reads article data from a central database (Google Sheets)
  • Processes multiple articles at once
  • Uses AI to analyze patterns across content
  • Generates structured output:
    • Top trends
    • Important topics
    • Business opportunities
    • Summary of insights
  • Automatically generates a ready-to-use report (Google Docs)
AUTOMATION

🔄 Make.com Scenario

Fully automated workflow that transforms raw data into high-level intelligence reports without manual effort.

BUSINESS VALUE

💡 Why this matters

  • Reduces research and analysis time by 80–90%
  • Detects trends before competitors
  • Turns raw data into strategic insights
  • Supports faster decision-making
  • Eliminates manual reporting
USE CASES

📈 Where businesses use this

  • Market intelligence
  • Trend monitoring
  • Startup & tech scouting
  • Investment research
  • Content strategy
TECH

🧠 Tech Stack

  • Make.com (automation)
  • Google Sheets (data source)
  • Google Gemini AI (analysis)
  • Google Docs (report generation)

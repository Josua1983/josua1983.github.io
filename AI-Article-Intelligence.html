🧠 AI Article Intelligence System

Automated system that transforms raw online content into structured, actionable business intelligence in real-time.

OVERVIEW

📊 What this system does

This system continuously monitors RSS feeds (e.g. TechCrunch), retrieves full article content, and uses AI to convert unstructured information into clear, structured insights.

Instead of manually reading articles, businesses receive summarized intelligence, categorized data, trend detection, and actionable signals — automatically.

PROCESS

⚙️ How it works

  • Monitors RSS feeds for new articles (real-time ingestion)
  • Fetches full article content via HTTP
  • Cleans and converts HTML into readable text
  • Uses AI to generate structured outputs:
    • Summary (3 sentences)
    • Category (AI, startup, marketing, etc.)
    • Trend detection (yes/no)
    • Importance score (1–10)
    • Business impact (low / medium / high)
    • Keywords extraction
  • Stores results in a structured database (Google Sheets)
  • Generates ready-to-use reports (Google Docs)
AUTOMATION

🔄 Make.com Scenario

Fully automated workflow built in Make.com. No manual input required — the system runs continuously.

BUSINESS VALUE

💡 Why this matters

  • Reduces manual research time by 80–90%
  • Transforms content overload into clear insights
  • Identifies trends before competitors
  • Enables faster, data-driven decisions
  • Creates a reusable intelligence database
USE CASES

📈 Where businesses use this

  • Market research automation
  • Startup & tech trend monitoring
  • Competitive intelligence
  • Content & media analysis
  • Investment research
TECH

🧠 Tech Stack

  • Make.com (automation engine)
  • RSS feeds (data ingestion)
  • HTTP module (content retrieval)
  • HTML parser (data cleaning)
  • Google Gemini AI (analysis)
  • Google Sheets (data storage)
  • Google Docs (report generation)

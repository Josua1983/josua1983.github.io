⚙️ AI Review Intelligence System

End-to-end AI system that collects, analyzes, and transforms customer reviews into actionable business insights automatically.

OVERVIEW

What this system does

This system collects customer reviews and transforms raw feedback into structured business intelligence using AI.

Instead of manually reading reviews, you instantly see what customers love, hate, and what needs improvement.

ARCHITECTURE

System architecture

The system runs as a two-layer pipeline: data collection and intelligence processing.

The first layer collects reviews from Google Maps using Apify.

The second layer processes everything with AI and extracts insights, patterns, and recommendations.

PROCESS

How it works

  • Collects reviews via Apify
  • Cleans and structures the data
  • Stores everything in a database
  • AI analyzes sentiment and patterns
  • Detects complaints and praise
  • Generates improvement insights
  • Creates automated reports
OUTPUT

What you get

  • Top positive drivers
  • Main complaints
  • Clear action points
  • Pricing & portion insights
  • Overall sentiment score
  • Automated reports

🤖 AI Lead Qualification & Routing System

AI-powered system that analyzes, classifies, and routes leads in real-time — turning incoming messages into immediate business actions.

OVERVIEW

🎯 What this system does

This system captures incoming leads and uses AI to instantly determine their value. Instead of manually reading and sorting messages, businesses receive structured insights and automatic routing.

High-value leads are acted on immediately, while lower-value leads are organized for follow-up — fully automated.

PROCESS

⚙️ How it works

  • Webhook receives lead data (name, email, message)
  • AI analyzes intent and classifies the lead
  • JSON parser structures the AI output
  • Router directs leads (HOT / WARM / COLD)
  • Telegram sends real-time notifications
AUTOMATION

🔄 Make.com Scenario

Fully automated pipeline that processes leads in real-time without manual intervention.

AI OUTPUT

🧠 Lead Intelligence

Each lead is enriched with structured insights:

  • Type → HOT / WARM / COLD
  • Summary → What the lead wants
  • Urgency → How fast to act
  • Intent → Buying vs browsing

This turns raw messages into clear business signals.

ALERTS

📲 Real-Time Notifications

Leads are instantly delivered to Telegram with actionable insights.

BUSINESS VALUE

💰 Why this matters

  • ⚡ Respond instantly to HOT leads → more conversions
  • 📉 Prevent lost revenue from slow replies
  • 🧠 No manual lead sorting
  • 📊 Scales with unlimited leads
  • 💸 Direct impact on sales performance
USE CASES

📈 Where businesses use this

  • Lead generation funnels
  • Marketing agencies
  • Sales teams
  • Service-based businesses
  • Startups handling inbound leads
TECH

🧠 Tech Stack

  • Make.com (automation)
  • Webhooks (lead intake)
  • Google Gemini AI (analysis)
  • JSON Parser (data structuring)
  • Telegram API (notifications)

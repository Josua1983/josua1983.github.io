🤖 AI Lead Qualification & Routing System

This system automatically processes incoming leads, analyzes intent using AI, and routes them instantly to the right channel. It replaces manual lead sorting and ensures businesses never miss high-value opportunities.

---

🔧 System Overview

The system is built using Make.com and consists of a real-time automation pipeline:

  • Webhook receives incoming lead data
  • AI (Google Gemini) analyzes the message
  • JSON parser structures the output
  • Router directs leads based on classification
  • Telegram sends instant notifications
---

🧠 Step 1 — Lead Input

A webhook captures incoming lead data such as:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Message

This can come from a website form, landing page, or API.

---

🧠 Step 2 — AI Analysis

The system uses AI to analyze the lead message and determine:

  • Type: HOT / WARM / COLD
  • Summary
  • Urgency
  • Intent
Example:

HOT → ready to buy
WARM → interested but not urgent
COLD → early research phase
---

🧩 Step 3 — JSON Structuring

The AI output is converted into structured data using a JSON parser. This ensures reliable routing and eliminates parsing errors.

---

🚦 Step 4 — Smart Routing

The router sends leads to different flows:

  • 🔥 HOT → Immediate notification
  • 🌤️ WARM → Follow-up flow
  • ❄️ COLD → Stored for later nurturing

A fallback route ensures no lead is ever lost.

---

📲 Step 5 — Real-Time Alerts

Leads are instantly delivered to Telegram with structured insights:

  • Summary of the situation
  • Intent of the lead
  • Urgency level

This enables immediate action without opening dashboards.

---

💰 Business Value

  • ⚡ Respond to HOT leads instantly → more conversions
  • 📉 Eliminate missed opportunities
  • 🧠 No manual lead sorting needed
  • 📊 Scalable system for high lead volume
  • 💸 Direct impact on revenue
Most businesses lose leads because they respond too late. This system fixes that completely.
---

🚀 Conclusion

This system transforms raw incoming leads into actionable insights and immediate actions. It turns AI into a real business tool that drives revenue instead of just generating data.

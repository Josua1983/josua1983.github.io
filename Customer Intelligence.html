🧠 AI Article Intelligence System

Automatically turn RSS articles into AI-powered summaries, categories and reports.

OVERVIEW

📊 What this system does

This automation monitors RSS feeds (like TechCrunch), extracts full article content, and uses AI to generate structured summaries with categories and scoring.

PROCESS

⚙️ How it works

  • Detects new articles from RSS feeds
  • Fetches full article content via HTTP
  • Converts HTML into clean readable text
  • Uses AI (Gemini) to summarize and categorize
  • Saves results into Google Sheets
  • Creates structured reports in Google Docs

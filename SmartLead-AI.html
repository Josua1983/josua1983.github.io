🤖 SmartLead AI

AI-powered system that captures, qualifies and converts incoming leads automatically — in real-time.

OVERVIEW

🎯 What this system does

SmartLead AI turns every incoming email into structured, actionable intelligence. Instead of manually reading and sorting messages, the system instantly classifies leads into HOT, WARM or SPAM and triggers the right action automatically. High-value leads are handled immediately, while lower-priority messages are organized for follow-up ensuring zero missed opportunities.

⚙️ How it works

  • Incoming email is captured via Gmail
  • AI analyzes intent, urgency and buying signals
  • Lead is classified (HOT / WARM / SPAM)
  • System routes and triggers actions automatically
  • Data is stored and updated inside Google Sheets (CRM)

🔄 Make.com Scenario

Built entirely in Make.com, this system connects Gmail, Gemini AI, Telegram and Google Sheets into one automated pipeline.

It runs 24/7, processes every incoming lead in real-time, and executes actions without any manual input.

🧠 Lead Intelligence

Each email is enriched with AI-generated insights:

  • Intent → what the lead actually wants
  • Urgency → how fast you should respond
  • Classification → HOT / WARM / SPAM
  • Reason → why the lead is categorized this way

🚨 Alerts

HOT leads trigger instant Telegram notifications so you can act immediately — while competitors are still reading their inbox.

No delays. No missed deals.

💰 Why this matters

Speed = revenue.

Most businesses lose 30–70% of leads simply because they respond too late.

SmartLead AI eliminates that problem completely by ensuring every serious lead gets immediate attention — while filtering out noise automatically.

The result: more conversions, less manual work, and direct revenue impact.
